WHAT IS MUSIC REELS?

Music Reels is the newest way to create video content on music hut to express your singing talent.You can record and edit a video of maximum 1 min .

PROCEDURE:

#Login your account on music hut

#Go to reels tab

#Choose the song or music you love from the music library

#Make a reel by using the features avilable

#Once your reel is live ,you can share it on your profile reels tab.

ADDITIONAL FEATURES:

1.You can use filters

2.Slow motion option is available

3.Video editing option is available

4.Multisnap option is available

The more people you reach with your reels,the higher your chances that follower count and engagement rate will grow.
